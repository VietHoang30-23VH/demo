About us


“Chess is hard”. While many see Chess as an elegant and intellectual game, involving mind-blowing tactics and countless hours mastering how to move 16 pieces, there’s a deeper layer waiting to be explored. Beyond the board, emotions come alive - glory, relief, anxiety, victory - as dedicated players immerse themselves in this timeless pursuit. It’s those feelings that makes Chess so attractive.
